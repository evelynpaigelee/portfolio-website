About

My Story

Hi, I'm Eve Lee! I'm UI/UX designer passionate about crafting thoughtful, beautiful solutions with user experience and design that foster genuine connections. While my transition from healthcare to design might seem unconventional, I've always been drawn to the beauty and functionality of design in art, technology, or everyday life. Embracing this new path, I decided to take the leap into UI/UX design.

My healthcare background has equipped me with skills in interdisciplinary collaboration, empathy, and problem-solving, enabling me to guide people toward meaningful solutions. I strive to create engaging and impactful designs that meet the needs of users, whether in a hospital setting or through innovative products. When I'm not designing, you can find me baking, hiking, throwing pottery, or enjoying delicious meals with family and friends.
